Welcome!

We warmly welcome you to our fellowship of believers who gather in the name of the Lord Jesus Christ. We are simply a community of Christians devoted to honoring God's Word as our final authority for faith and practice and to growing together in the grace and knowledge of Christ.

Our desire is to worship God in spirit and in truth, following the pattern of the New Testament church through teaching, fellowship, breaking of bread, and prayer, as we encourage one another toward spiritual maturity in love.

All ages are cherished here, and we rejoice to include children as an integral part of our church family. With open arms and reverent hearts, we invite you to join us as we seek to exalt the Lord Jesus Christ and be built up together in His grace and truth.

Our Foundation

Built upon the unchanging Word of God and the rich heritage of the Open Brethren movement

A Journey of Faith

Stafford Bible Chapel began in November 2014 through the vision of Dr. Mathew Vairamon, starting humbly in a family home and growing into a thriving community of believers. From Murphy Road to our current location at 10502 Altonbury Lane, God has been faithful in establishing this work.

As an Indian Brethren Assembly, we cherish the simplicity of New Testament Christianity. We gather without hierarchical structures, honoring the priesthood of all believers while being led by godly elders. Our worship is characterized by reverence, authenticity, and the centrality of Christ.

Located in the heart of Stafford—the vibrant center of Houston's Malayalee community— we serve families from diverse backgrounds with services in both English and Malayalam, creating a bridge between traditional faith and contemporary life.
