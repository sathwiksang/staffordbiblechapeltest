Our Story

Founded in Faith, Growing in Grace

Stafford Bible Chapel was founded in November 2014 by Dr. Mathew Vairamon, beginning with just one family gathering in his home. Through God's blessing, our congregation has grown and moved from Murphy Road to our current location at 10502 Altonbury Lane, Houston, TX 77031.

Situated at the border of Stafford City, we serve the vibrant Malayalam community in Houston. Stafford is surrounded by Sugar Land, Missouri City, Rosenberg, Richmond, Pearland, and Houston, making it the heart of Malayalam culture in the area.
