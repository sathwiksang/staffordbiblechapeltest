Established 2014

Our Story & Mission

Stafford Bible Chapel began in November 2014 through the vision and dedication of Dr. Mathew Vairamon. What started as a single family gathering in a home has grown into a vibrant community of believers from the Indian diaspora and beyond.

From our humble beginnings, we moved to Murphy Road in Stafford before establishing our current location at 10502 Altonbury Lane, Houston, TX 77031. Situated at the border of Stafford City, we serve the spiritual needs of the large Malayalee community in this area, often considered the capital of Malayalees in Houston.

We are an assembly of believers gathering solely in the name of the Lord Jesus Christ, holding fast to New Testament principles of worship and fellowship. Our meetings are conducted in both English and Malayalam, welcoming all who seek to know Christ.

What We Believe

Our Statement of Faith

The Holy Scriptures

We believe the Bible is the divinely inspired Word of God, verbally inspired and the final authority for all faith and practice.

The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three equally divine Persons: the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus Christ is the eternal Son of God, born of the virgin Mary, who died for our sins and rose again for our justification.

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone, not by works, and is the free gift of God to all who believe.

The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ, composed of all believers, gathering in His name according to New Testament principles.

The Second Coming

We believe in the imminent return of our Lord Jesus Christ to receive His Church and establish His kingdom.
