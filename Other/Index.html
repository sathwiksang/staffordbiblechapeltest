A Journey of Faith

Stafford Bible Chapel began in November 2014 through the vision of Dr. Mathew Vairamon, starting humbly in a family home and growing into a thriving community of believers. From Murphy Road to our current location at 10502 Altonbury Lane, God has been faithful in establishing this work.

As an Indian Brethren Church, we cherish the simplicity of New Testament Christianity. We gather without hierarchical structures, honoring the priesthood of all believers while being led by godly elders. Our worship is characterized by reverence, authenticity, and the centrality of Christ.

Located in the heart of Stafford—the vibrant center of Houston's Malayalee community— we serve families from diverse backgrounds with services in both English and Malayalam, creating a bridge between traditional faith and contemporary life.

Dr. Mathew Vairamon

Founding Elder

By God's grace, Dr. Vairamon pioneered this work in 2014, beginning with a burden to establish a New Testament church that would serve the growing Indian community in Stafford with faithfulness to Scripture.

Church Eldership

Pastoral Oversight

Following the Biblical pattern of Acts 14:23, our church is guided by a plurality of elders who provide spiritual oversight, teaching, and pastoral care to the congregation.

Servant Leadership

Ministry Team

In keeping with the priesthood of all believers, various members serve in different capacities—from children's ministry to hospitality—each using their gifts for the edification of the body.
