About Our Church

Built on the foundation of Scripture and guided by the principles of the Open Brethren tradition

Our Heritage

Stafford Bible Chapel began in November 2014 through the vision of Dr. Mathew Vairamon. Starting as a house church with a single family, we have grown into a vibrant community of believers gathered at 10502 Altonbury Lane, Houston, TX 77031.

As an Indian Brethren Church, we hold fast to the simplicity of New Testament worship, the authority of Scripture, and the priesthood of all believers. We conduct our services in both English and Malayalam, serving the diverse Malayalee community in Stafford.

Located in the border of Stafford City, we serve a community rich in Malayalee culture, surrounded by Sugar Land, Missouri City, Rosenberg, Richmond, Pearland, and Houston.

Worship & Fellowship

Join us in worship, prayer, and the study of God's Word throughout the week

⛪

Sunday Worship

Our main worship service begins at 9:00 AM every Sunday, featuring expository preaching, congregational singing, and the Lord's Supper in the traditional Brethren manner.

👥

Friday Sister's Meeting

Ladies gather every Friday morning for prayer, Bible study, and Christian fellowship, strengthening our sisterhood in Christ through mutual encouragement and support.

📖

Saturday Prayer & Bible Study

Every Saturday at 7:00 PM, we come together for focused prayer and verse-by-verse Bible study, deepening our understanding of God's Word.

👶

Sunday School

Children's ministry takes place on Sunday after the morning worship service, providing age-appropriate Bible teaching and Christian education.
