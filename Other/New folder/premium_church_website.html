Our Sacred Foundation

Built Upon the Rock of Ages

In November 2014, by God's divine providence, Dr. Mathew Vairamon was called to establish Stafford Bible Chapel. What began as a single family's faithful commitment to worship has grown into a thriving spiritual community of over 200 families.

From our humble beginnings in a home on Murphy Road to our current sanctuary at 10502 Altonbury Lane, we have witnessed the Lord's faithfulness in every season. Our church stands as a testament to His grace and provision.

"Unless the Lord builds the house, the builders labor in vain." — Psalm 127:1

Located at the heart of Stafford City, we joyfully serve the Malayalam community throughout Houston, Sugar Land, Missouri City, Pearland, and the greater metropolitan area. Our doors are open to all who seek to know Christ and grow in His love.
