Our Foundation

Built on Faith, Growing in Grace

Stafford Bible Chapel was founded in November 2014 by Dr. Mathew Vairamon, beginning with one family's commitment to worship. Through God's faithfulness, we have grown from a home fellowship to a thriving church community.

Located at 10502 Altonbury Lane in Houston, we serve the heart of the Malayalam community at the border of Stafford City. Our church welcomes families from Sugar Land, Missouri City, Pearland, and throughout the greater Houston area.
