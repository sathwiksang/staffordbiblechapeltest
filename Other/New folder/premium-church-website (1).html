Since 2014

A Sacred Space for Spiritual Growth

Founded by Dr. Mathew Vairamon, Stafford Bible Chapel began as a humble gathering in a home with one family. Today, we stand as a beacon of faith for Houston's Malayalam Christian community.

Our congregation gathers at the crossroads of Houston's diverse communities - Stafford, Sugar Land, Missouri City, and beyond - united by our shared faith and commitment to biblical truth.

We believe in the transformative power of God's Word, the strength found in authentic community, and the calling to serve others with Christ's love. Every Sunday, we gather to worship in both Malayalam and English, creating a bridge between tradition and contemporary faith.
