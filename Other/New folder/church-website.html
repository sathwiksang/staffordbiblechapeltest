Our Sacred Story

A Gathering of Faith

Founded in 2014 by Dr. Mathew Vairamon, Stafford Bible Chapel emerged from a simple vision: to create a sanctuary where souls could gather in authentic worship and deep biblical study.

What began as intimate home gatherings has blossomed into a vibrant congregation, united by our shared devotion to Christ and commitment to spiritual growth.

As an Indian Brethren Assembly, we honor our heritage while embracing all who seek truth. Our bilingual services in English and Malayalam reflect the beautiful diversity of God's kingdom.

Our Ministries

Children's Ministry

Nurturing young hearts through engaging biblical lessons, creative activities, and age-appropriate worship that builds a strong foundation of faith.

Women's Fellowship

A sacred space for women to gather in prayer, dive deep into Scripture, and build lasting bonds of sisterhood in Christ.

Bible Study Groups

Systematic exploration of God's Word through verse-by-verse study, theological discussion, and practical life application.

Prayer Ministry

Dedicated intercessors lifting up our church, community, and world through fervent prayer and spiritual warfare.

Youth Ministry

Dynamic programs for teens and young adults, fostering spiritual growth, authentic relationships, and bold faith for the next generation.

Community Outreach

Extending Christ's love beyond our walls through compassionate service, practical support, and Gospel witness to Houston.

Our Leadership

Dr. Mathew Vairamon

FOUNDING PASTOR

Called to shepherd God's people with wisdom and grace, Dr. Mathew Vairamon founded Stafford Bible Chapel in 2014 with a vision for authentic biblical community. His expository preaching, pastoral heart, and unwavering commitment to God's Word have been instrumental in building our church family. Under his leadership, we've grown from a small home gathering into a thriving congregation united in worship and service.
