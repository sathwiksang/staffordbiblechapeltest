Welcome to Our Church Family

A Heritage of Faith Since 2014

Stafford Bible Chapel began as a vision of faith and community in November 2014, when Dr. Mathew Vairamon opened his home to worship with a single family. Through God's grace and the dedication of our growing congregation, we have flourished into a vibrant spiritual community.

From our humble beginnings in a family home to our previous location on Murphy Road, we now gather at our current sanctuary on Altonbury Lane. Our journey reflects the power of faith to bring people together and create lasting bonds of fellowship.

Located in the heart of Stafford, we serve a diverse community where English and Malayalam languages unite us in worship. Our church stands as a beacon of faith in this vibrant area, surrounded by Sugar Land, Missouri City, and the greater Houston metropolitan area.

Our Ministries

Serving Our Community with Love

At Stafford Bible Chapel, we believe in serving our community through various ministries that strengthen faith, build relationships, and spread God's love. Our programs are designed to meet the spiritual needs of all ages and backgrounds.

Children's Ministry

Our Sunday School program provides age-appropriate biblical teaching, helping children grow in their understanding of God's word and love.

Women's Fellowship

The Friday Sister's Meeting provides a nurturing environment for women to grow spiritually, build friendships, and support one another in faith.

Bible Study

Join us every Saturday evening for in-depth Bible study and prayer, as we explore Scripture together and grow in our understanding of God's truth.

Community Outreach

We actively serve the Malayalam community in the greater Houston area, providing spiritual support and fellowship to families seeking a church home.

Church Leadership

Faithful Servants Called to Lead

Dr. Mathew Vairamon

Founding Pastor

Dr. Mathew Vairamon founded Stafford Bible Chapel in November 2014, beginning this ministry of faith with just his family in his own home. Through his vision and dedication, what started as a small gathering has grown into a thriving church community.

Under his pastoral leadership, the church has moved from a house fellowship to a rental facility and finally to our current location, always maintaining the core values of biblical teaching, community fellowship, and spiritual growth.

Our Community

Stafford Bible Chapel is situated at the border of Stafford City, a vibrant suburban area of Houston. Our location makes us easily accessible to families from Sugar Land, Missouri City, Rosenberg, Richmond, Pearland, and greater Houston.

Stafford is home to a large Malayalam-speaking community and is considered the heart of Malayalee culture in the Houston area. Many families travel here for worship, fellowship, and community activities.
