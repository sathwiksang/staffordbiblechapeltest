Our Journey of Faith

Stafford Bible Chapel began in November 2014 with a single family's vision, started by Dr. Mathew Vairamon in his home. As our spiritual family grew, we moved to a rental space and now gather at 10502 Altonbury Lane, Houston, TX 77031.

Nestled in Stafford, the heart of Houston's Malayalee community, we are a fellowship that cherishes the clear teaching of God's Word in both English and Malayalam, striving to be a spiritual home for all.
