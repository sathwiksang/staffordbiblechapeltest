Our Story

Stafford Bible Chapel began its journey in November 2014, founded by Dr. Mathew Vairamon with a vision to serve the Indian Brethren community in the greater Houston area. What started as a single family gathering in a home has blossomed into a vibrant congregation united in faith and fellowship.

Our church family has grown from humble beginnings on Murphy Road to our current location at 10502 Altonbury Lane, Houston, TX 77031. Positioned at the border of Stafford City, we serve the diverse Malayalam-speaking community that calls this area home.

Stafford has become the heart of the Malayalam community in Houston, with numerous churches, restaurants, and cultural centers. We are blessed to be part of this rich tapestry, offering worship services in both English and Malayalam to bridge generations and cultures in Christ's love.

Our Leadership

Shepherding God's Flock with Dedication

Dr. Mathew Vairamon

Founder & Pastor

With a heart for serving the Malayalam-speaking community in Houston, Dr. Vairamon founded Stafford Bible Chapel in 2014. His vision continues to guide our fellowship as we grow together in faith and unity. Under his leadership, our church has become a spiritual home for many families in the Fort Bend and Harris County areas.

Our Faith

Standing Firm on Biblical Truth

We are a Bible-believing church committed to the fundamental truths of Scripture. Our fellowship is rooted in the Brethren tradition, emphasizing the priesthood of all believers, the authority of God's Word, and the simplicity of New Testament worship.

We gather in the name of our Lord Jesus Christ, celebrating the Lord's Supper weekly, practicing believer's baptism, and encouraging every member to use their spiritual gifts for the edification of the body.
