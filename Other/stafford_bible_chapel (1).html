Our Heritage

Founded in Faith, Growing in Grace

Stafford Bible Chapel began its blessed journey in November 2014 under the visionary leadership of Dr. Mathew Vairamon. What commenced as a humble gathering of a single family in a home has blossomed into a vibrant congregation serving the Greater Houston area, particularly the thriving Malayalee community of Stafford.

Our assembly has grown from its initial location on Murphy Road to our current sanctuary at 10502 Altonbury Lane, strategically positioned at the border of Stafford City. This area, surrounded by Sugar Land, Missouri City, Rosenberg, Richmond, Pearland, and Houston, has become the spiritual heart for many Indian families seeking to worship according to New Testament principles.

We gather solely in the name of the Lord Jesus Christ, maintaining the apostolic traditions of the early church. Our services are conducted in both English and Malayalam, creating a bridge between generations and cultures while remaining anchored in biblical truth.

Our Foundation

What We Believe

Holy Scriptures

We believe the Bible is the divinely inspired, verbally given Word of God, infallible and the final authority for all matters of faith and practice.

The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three equally divine Persons: the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit, each possessing the same nature and attributes.

Lord Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus Christ is the eternal Son of God, born of the virgin Mary, who lived a sinless life, died for our sins, rose bodily from the dead, and is coming again.

Salvation by Grace

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone, not by works, and that all who trust in Him receive eternal life as a free gift.

The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ, composed of all believers, gathering in His name according to New Testament principles for worship, fellowship, and service.

Christ's Return

We believe in the imminent, personal return of our Lord Jesus Christ to receive His Church and establish His millennial kingdom on earth.
