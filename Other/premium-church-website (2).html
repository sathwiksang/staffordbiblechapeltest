Our Foundation in Christ

A Fellowship Rooted in God's Word Since 2014

"Not forsaking the assembling of ourselves together, as the manner of some is; but exhorting one another" - Hebrews 10:25

In November 2014, Dr. Mathew Vairamon responded to the Lord's calling to establish a Bible-centered assembly in Stafford. Beginning with one family gathering in a home for prayer and the study of God's Word, the Lord has faithfully grown this fellowship into a testimony of His grace among the Indian Brethren community in Houston.

"And they continued stedfastly in the apostles' doctrine and fellowship" - Acts 2:42

From Murphy Road to our current gathering at 10502 Altonbury Lane, Houston, TX 77031, we have witnessed God's faithfulness. Located at the border of Stafford City, we serve as a spiritual home for Malayalam-speaking believers from Sugar Land, Missouri City, Rosenberg, Richmond, Pearland, and throughout Fort Bend and Harris Counties.

"That they all may be one; as thou, Father, art in me, and I in thee" - John 17:21

Stafford has become the heart of the Malayalam community in Houston. We gather to worship in both English and Malayalam, uniting generations in the truth of Scripture and the simplicity of New Testament fellowship.

Dr. Mathew Vairamon

Founder & Elder

Called by the Lord to establish this assembly in November 2014, Dr. Vairamon has faithfully served the Malayalam-speaking believers in Houston. His burden for the spiritual growth of Indian Brethren families has been the foundation of this fellowship. Beginning with one family gathering for prayer, the Lord has used his ministry to build a congregation that stands on the authority of Scripture and the simplicity of New Testament worship. The assembly continues to grow under his care, bridging generations through bilingual ministry and maintaining the biblical pattern of gathering established by the early church.
